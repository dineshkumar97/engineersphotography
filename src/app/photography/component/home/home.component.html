Wedding candid photography is a style of capturing moments during a wedding in a natural, unposed, and spontaneous manner. The goal is to document genuine emotions, expressions, and interactions, rather than relying on staged or formal poses. Candid wedding photographers aim to blend into the background, allowing them to capture the authentic essence of the event without disrupting the flow or atmosphere.
